About AgriVision 🌱

AgriVision is a smart agricultural support system designed to assist farmers in making data-driven decisions for better crop yield and disease management.

It analyzes important factors like soil nutrients (NPK), pH level, temperature, humidity, and rainfall to recommend the most suitable crops.

The system also includes a leaf disease detection feature using image processing, helping farmers identify plant diseases at an early stage and reduce crop loss.

AgriVision is simple, user-friendly, and accessible on both computers and mobile devices, making it a helpful tool for modern agriculture.
